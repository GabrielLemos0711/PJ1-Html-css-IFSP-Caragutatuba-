In addition to the beaches being a great destination for those who want to have fun, relax and exercise, it is proven that sea water has several benefits for our health. Whether breathing, skin or even disease prevention, the sea is a great ally to maintain a healthy life. Check out some of the benefits present in salt water.



  1. Decreases tension and stress

    2. In addition to the act of going to the beach itself being relaxing and fun, a bath in the sea can make everything much better. Because it is rich in magnesium and other substances beneficial to our body, sea water relieves joint and muscle tension, consequently reducing stress and anxiety.

  2. Strengthens the immune system

    3. According to a study carried out in Spain by the University of Alicante, sea water contains sodium chloride, as well as other substances that are important in the development of the best functioning of our cells, increasing immunity and strengthening the body against viruses and bacteria, helping , therefore, in the prevention of diseases.

  3. Skin healing and treatment

    4. In addition to improving our immunity, salt water also helps in better wound healing. The mineral salts present in the water have an antibiotic effect, helping to relieve and heal several cases of skin diseases, from simple wounds or inflammations, to some more serious ones such as dermatitis. A bath in the sea also eliminates accumulated dead skin, providing a beneficial light scaling, being a natural and powerful resource for the treatment of psoriasis and dandruff.

  4. improves breathing

    5. Just breathing the sea breeze already has its proven benefits. A study carried out in Australia revealed that inhaling the breeze relieves sinusitis and coughing because of the sodium chloride solution present. When bathing, salt water contributes to the elimination of toxins, as well as foreign elements present in the lungs.

  5. Helps with kidney or liver problems

    6. Seawater helps treat liver conditions such as cirrhosis by speeding up the regeneration of cells that have been damaged. It also helps in eliminating excess water that accumulates in the abdomen due to illness.
